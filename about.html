about – i'm from toronto and i love sushi and brooklyn nine-nine.

I'm currently in my first year of Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I was drawn to design because of its' emphasis on human interaction and how it creatively accommodates for our intrinsic behaviours. I love tech but algorithms and machines will never replace our consciousness, passion and emotions. Tech is a tool that makes our lives more productive, and the ease of our touch points with it is what makes it so useful.

I'm always listening to Jeremy Zucker and I will never say no to a game of ultimate frisbee or Avalon. Recently, I made a promise to myself to constantly be doing something on the side to improve myself. I'm currently completing the 100 DailyUI challenge, reading anything design and business related I can find, and occasionally writing about my thoughts.
