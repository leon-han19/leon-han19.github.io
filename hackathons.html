uXperience | ThinkPrivacy Design Jam

Clubhouse – 1st place

This project was submitted to the uXperience | ThinkPrivacy Design Jam, hosted by the University of Waterloo.

Our team of 5 was tasked with creating a privacy-conscious experience that helped create a sense of togetherness.

As high school students not too long ago who thrived through extracurricular activities and clubs, we focused on creating a safe space for students to express their passions and find other students to share it with. By providing this space, students can connect with current friends and find new people to bond over mutual interests.

Activision + Adobe Creative Jam

Pebble – 3rd place

This project was submitted to the College + Activision Adobe Creative Jam; we won 3rd place out of 300+ teams! Feel free to play with the prototype. →

My team and I designed Pebble to help adults step into the gaming world. As non-gaming adults, we leveraged our unique perspective to accomodate for adults who might want to start gaming but are intimidated by the community and don't know where to start.

This was a fun project because it gave us lots of flexibility to think of creative solutions and it was our first time designing for dark mode!

Airbnb + Adobe Creative Jam

Scrapbooks

This project was a part of the College + Airbnb Creative Jam hosted by Adobe.

My partner and I designed Airbnb Scrapbooks to help groups of people document and share their stays in a creative, scrapbook-like way.

Our main focus was on the branding and impact of features on the user rather than the features themselves. Some questions we kept in mind throughout were: how can we get people excited to use this? What's going to make them want to use and share this feature?

Starterhacks 2020

Fitbud

Fitbud is a web application to help people of all experience levels find gym buddies. I designed and developed the front-end with a team of 4 others.

Going to the gym by yourself can be daunting, especially if you're a newbie or in a new place. Our app not only helps people overcome this obstacle and allows them to achieve their fitness goals, but also connects people and helps form lasting relationships.
